# Ways of Working

## Epic/Issue model

Enabled.

**Epic**: A time-bound body of work with an owner, a target date, and multiple
child issues. Epics are the unit of planning and reporting. In Linear, an Epic
is a parent issue labeled `epic`. An Epic must have: a clear goal, a why, an
owner, and a target date. An Epic must not be: a single issue disguised as a
goal, open-ended, or unowned.

**Issue**: A concrete unit of work that one person can complete. Issues may be
standalone or children of an Epic. Sub-issues are just Issues with a parent.

**Principle**: Engineering and stakeholders communicate at the Epic level.
Stakeholders define goals (what and why); engineering breaks them into Issues
(how). Status reporting is always at the Epic level.
